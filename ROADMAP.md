# 🗺️ LoneWriter Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the future of LoneWriter. We prioritize features based on user impact, technical feasibility, and our absolute privacy philosophy.

## 🟢 High Priority
- **[ ] Mobile PWA Polish**: Improving touch gestures and offline reliability for writing on the go.
- **[ ] Interactive Timelines**: A visual way to map out events, character arcs, and world history chronologically.
- **[ ] Zettelkasten System**: Integrated 3D knowledge graph in the Compendium to visualize connections between entities.

## 🟡 Low Priority
- **[ ] Extended Import**: Support for direct parsing of .pdf and .docx documents into the application.
- **[ ] MCP Reference Integration**: Read reference files and auto-extract data via MCP to feed the Compendium.
- **[ ] Smart Bootstrap**: Map and import Markdown files directly into the narrative structure tree.

## ⚫ Ideas to Consider
*(These are NOT planned but are being explored based on interest)*
- **[ ] Anaphora Optimization**: Refined coherence and better resolution for long narrative texts.
- **[ ] Enhanced RAG Memory**: Support for larger local models and better indexing of massive world-building files.
- **[ ] Narrative Continuity Engine v2**: Beyond just consistency, the AI will proactively suggest plot connections based on established lore.
- **[ ] Advanced Export (EPUB/PDF)**: Professional formatting for digital publishing and printing, including customizable stylesheets.
- **[ ] Collaborative Mode (Peer Review)**: Share a read-only link or a "comment-only" version of your manuscript with editors or beta readers.
- **[ ] Pacing Analysis**: Visual insights into the emotional arc and pacing of your scenes to identify "slow" or "flat" spots.

---

*Note: This roadmap is a living document that evolves with the feedback of our community on Reddit.*
