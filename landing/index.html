MPC Coreference Engine

Minimum Plausible Context

LoneWriter extracts and categorizes important elements dynamically as you type without consuming massive amounts of AI tokens.

  • Characters: Tracks character traits and appearances dynamically.
  • Locations: Connects scenes directly to your custom world map locales.
  • Lore & Objects: Contextual awareness of your magical or sci-fi systems.
  • Saliency Scoring: Injects only the most relevant lore into your AI prompts.
